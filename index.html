Simple Blocks & Pipeline Example with Interaction

Made by Jisang Ha & Henrique M. Gaspar ~ August 2024

This is an example of blocks and a pipeline that responds to user's drag.

It gets data from GunnerusData.js and GunnerusConnection.js.

This example gets information about the relationship of the target objects that need to be connected and the diameter of the pipe from the Connection data, and the size and location of the target objects from the data.

It visualizes the objects based on the information obtained and creates the shortest pipeline connecting the centers of the objects.

By draging, user can rearrange objects' location, and pipelines connecting objects are recreated in real-time.
